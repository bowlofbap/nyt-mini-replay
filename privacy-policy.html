Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 7, 2025

Overview

NYT Crossword Replay ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome extension collects, uses, and safeguards information when you use our service.

Information We Collect

Crossword Solving Data

Our extension records the following information during your crossword solving sessions:

  • Letters typed and deleted in crossword cells
  • Cell selection and navigation patterns
  • Timing data for each action
  • Puzzle completion status
  • Grid structure and size information

Technical Data

We may collect:

  • Browser type and version
  • Extension usage patterns
  • Error logs for debugging purposes

Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, address)
  • Payment information
  • Browsing history outside of NYT crossword pages
  • Any data from websites other than nytimes.com crossword pages

How We Use Your Information

The collected data is used solely to:

  • Create replay recordings of your crossword solving sessions
  • Generate shareable replay links
  • Improve extension functionality and user experience
  • Diagnose and fix technical issues

Data Storage and Security

Your solving recordings are:

  • Stored securely in Firebase/Firestore with encryption in transit and at rest
  • Identified by random, non-identifiable IDs
  • Accessible only through the specific replay links you choose to share
  • Not linked to your personal identity in any way

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your data to third parties. Replay recordings are only accessible to:

  • People you share replay links with
  • Our technical team for debugging purposes (anonymized data only)

Data Retention

Crossword replay recordings are stored indefinitely to ensure shared links continue to work. However, since recordings contain no personal information, this does not impact your privacy.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Stop using the extension at any time
  • Request information about data we have collected
  • Request deletion of specific recordings (contact us with the replay ID)

Third-Party Services

We use Google Firebase/Firestore for data storage. Please review Google's Privacy Policy for information about their data handling practices.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

Children's Privacy

Our extension is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through our GitHub repository issues page.

Compliance

This extension complies with:

  • Google Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies
  • General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) where applicable
  • California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) where applicable
